Web Dev Task 1

Q1. Which part of the interview surprised you the most?

The fact that they asked me to sing in a design interview surprised me the most. I never expected to go it this way.

Q2. What was the most memorable moment?

After my singing Ayush Bhaiya, the president came out of nowhere and said "You sing very well, you're already into the club!"

Q3. Which moment are you proud of?

After my interview I was assigned a task to make a poster. Even though I was new to design I completed the task way before the deadline and seniors were happy for me being punctual.

Q4. Rate your interview on a scale of 1 to 10.

10/10, bruh. No question!

Q5. Something that the interviewer should have done differently.

I liked the fun and chill environment during the interview. But, personally I felt that the amount of informal talks could have been brought down slightly, then the interview would have been more formal.
